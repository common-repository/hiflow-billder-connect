This plug-in allows to connect your Woocommerce website to your Billder or Hiflow account.

== Description ==

Billder and Hiflow are Saas application that allow you to manage your activity. The main features are :

- Customers and suppliers management
- Invoice and estimate management
- Link with accounting software
- Reporting
- And many more


With this plugin you can generate customers and invoices directly in your billder/hiflow account when a payment is accepted.

It will also link the invoice created in your billder/hiflow account in the order created in woocommerce.

This plugin require a Billder or Hiflow account. If you don't have an account, create it online at : https://www.billder.net or https://www.hiflow.net

You can find out our privacy policies here :  [billder.net/privacy](https://www.billder.net/fr/privacy) or [hiflow.net/privacy](https://www.hiflow.net/fr/privacy).
